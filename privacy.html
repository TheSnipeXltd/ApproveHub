Privacy

Demo documentation page for ApproveHub.

Overview

This is a static demo site. Data you enter is stored locally in your browser using localStorage.

What we store

  • Demo records: jobs, payees, invoices, milestones, releases, disputes, messages, audit log
  • UI preferences: theme and role switcher selection

What we don’t do

  • No server-side storage
  • No tracking pixels or analytics in this demo
We do not hold client funds. Funds are held and released by our escrow/PBA partner.
